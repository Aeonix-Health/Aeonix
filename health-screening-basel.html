Health Screening in Basel: Preventive Blood Testing for Basel Residents

2026-04-06  ·  5 min read

If you live or work in Basel, you already know that preventive healthcare in Switzerland requires navigating the gap between what your Krankenkasse covers and what a genuinely comprehensive health assessment looks like. Aeonix is designed to close that gap — a fully digital, Swiss-certified blood screening service that delivers 100 to 150+ biomarkers with physician-reviewed results in 48 hours.

Why Basel residents choose Aeonix

Pharma capital — Novartis, Roche, Lonza HQ. The health demands on the people who live and work here are significant. Chronic stress, long hours, international travel, and the pressures of high-performance careers create a specific risk profile that a standard GP check-up — measuring 12–20 markers — simply does not address.

How it works in Basel

Aeonix works with accredited Swiss partner laboratories. After registering and selecting your plan, you receive a test requisition. You attend a local certified collection point — in or near Basel — for a routine blood draw. No prior appointment with a physician required. Results are delivered to your secure personal dashboard within 48 hours, with a physician commentary explaining every value in clinical context.

What Aeonix tests in Basel

  • Cardiovascular risk: ApoB, Lp(a), full lipid panel, hsCRP, IL-6
  • Metabolic health: Fasting insulin, HOMA-IR, HbA1c, fasting glucose
  • Hormonal health: Full thyroid panel (TSH, free T3, free T4, Anti-TPO), sex hormones, DHEA-S, cortisol
  • Nutritional status: Vitamin D, Omega-3 Index, B12, folate, iron, ferritin, zinc, magnesium
  • Cancer adjunct markers: PSA (men), CA-125 (women), CEA (Longevity Plus and Elite plans)

Pricing for Basel residents

Plans start at CHF 595/year (35 biomarkers) on an annual subscription. The Vital Edge plan at CHF 795/year includes 60 biomarkers and Lp(a). The Longevity Plus and Elite plans extend to 80 and 100+ biomarkers respectively. All plans include physician review, digital results dashboard, and year-on-year longitudinal tracking.

Multilingual support

Aeonix supports English, German, French and Italian — across the website, results commentary, and customer support. For Basel's international community, this is a meaningful difference from Swiss GP appointments where the consultation language may not be your strongest.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a doctor's referral? No. Aeonix operates as a direct-access private screening service.

Is it covered by my Krankenkasse? KVG basic insurance does not cover routine preventive blood screening for healthy adults. Some supplementary (VVG) plans include a prevention budget — check your policy.

How long does the blood draw take? Approximately 10–15 minutes at the collection point.

When do I get my results? Within 48 hours of your blood draw, delivered to your secure dashboard.
